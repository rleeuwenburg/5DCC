Pigs in a Blanket

Even pigs get cold. So we'll wrap them in blankets and add some spice for extra hotness!

Ingredients

  • 250 gr. Cocktail Franks
  • 250 gr. Smoked bacon strips
  • Maple Syrup
  • Pepper, freshly grounded from peppermill

For the dip

  • 100 ml. Mayonaise
  • 2 teaspoons Mustard
  • Cayenne pepper (powdered)
  • Smoked paprika (powdered)

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 180 deg.C.
  2. Cut bacon to strips that are just long enough to wrap once around the mini sausage.
  3. Brush some maple syrup on inside of bacon strip and grind some pepper over it.
  4. Wrap sausage in bacon strip (with maple syrup on the inside) and place seamside down on a baking tray.
  5. Bake for 15-20 minutes until the bacon is crisp and slightly browned.
  6. For the dip, mix the mayo and mustard together until smooth then sprinkle with the smoked paprika and cayenne.
  7. Serve the sausages warm with the dip and Enjoy!

Extra Tip

Add pastry of your own choice to some of the pigs and create extra variety. Use baking paper on baking tray. It saves a lot of cleaning and gives the bacon a nice crisp.
